Nick B. Allen

Nick is a widely acclaimed author of rudimentary autobiographical websites. His most recent work, "Github Page for Nick Allen," is available at nickballen.github.io

Nick's first project will involve mapping the distribution of tetanus vaccination clinics in the United States, because his familiarity with geospatial tech is dangerously rusty.

One of Nick's favorite visualizations is this age-shaded map of Amersterdam's buildings. Nick is interested in representing built environments and their historical succession with time sliders and whatnot, which would require a database of vector objects tagged with construction and demolition dates. His efforts to do so are stunted by his weak abilities in image-based feature extraction and time-based visualization, as well as an acute lack of funding and time. Nick thinks this would be a pretty versatile way to examine contested processes of urban development and histories of disposession and urban expansion.
